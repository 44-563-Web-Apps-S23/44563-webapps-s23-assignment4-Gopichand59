VILLAIN.

It seems the devil himself can't take you! Neither your tongue nor your feet ever get tired. You get on my nerves. Sit down and shut up for a moment.

COMEDIAN.

_Me?_ Ha--ha! I'm going back there to the lady of my heart. I'll meet her and fetch her hither in my arms.

[_He spits on his hands, turns up his sleeves, and strides rapidly off toward the left._]

VILLAIN.

Clown!
